NANA


Synopsis:


Nana follows the life of two girls, both with the name of Nana. They meet on a train and compliment each others styles despite them being complete opposites. They continue their lives together in a shared apartment, having the best time with each other and having a closer bond over any lover. As the story develops, Nana Osaki and Nana Komatsu have problems, often about paying rent and getting money. The series, being unfinished, will never give fans closure after the unfortuante event that will unfold later into the story.
Character Name Description Appearance
Nana Osaki Nana Osaki is a very tough woman on the outside but if you get to know her deeply, she evolves from a very straight thinking woman to a very complex character. Deep down, all she desires is someone that she can love and trust and she ends up meeting Nana Komatsu, a true soulmate.
Nana Komatsu Nana Komatsu is a very bright and cheerful girl. Her very gullible heart allows very mentally destructive events to happen in the series. As she lives together with Nana Osaki, their relationship begins to grow until she meets a man who changes her life forever.
Junko Saotome Junko Saotome is a woman who knows what she wants and will not allow anyone to tell her what and what not to do. She meets her love interest later into the series and Junko and him both work together to help Nana Komatsu find a true man.
